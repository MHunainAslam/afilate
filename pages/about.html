Introducing Kinfolk, the app that helps you preserve your family memories

At Kinfolk, we believe that preserving family memories is the key to building stronger, more resilient families. Our app is designed to bring families together, promote greater understanding and communication, and ensure that your family's stories and traditions are never lost.

Here’s what we’ve done just for family.

With Kinfolk, you can track your family lineage, connect with family members near and far, and create a rich, interactive family history that captures your family's stories and traditions.

Great Stories are those that are told

Kinfolk has powerful storytelling tools, which allow you to capture and preserve your family memories in a way that's engaging, interactive, and fun. With Kinfolk, you can upload photos, videos, and documents, and use our intuitive timeline and storytelling features to create a visual and narrative record of your family history.
