Get started

Onoboading

Sign up for a new Kinfolk account, by providing your name, email address, and creating a password.

Create Profile

Create a Kinfolk profile by adding personal information, including a profile picture, bio, and any other details.

Connect with family

Use Kinfolk's "Add Folk" feature to connect with family members by sending them an invite link. Once connected, they can view their family members' profiles and start sharing memories.

Upload Memories

Upload photos, videos, documents, and audio recordings of their family memories to their Kinfolk profile. They can also add descriptions and tag family members in the memories.

Explore the timeline

The user navigates to the "Timeline" feature to view their family's history in chronological order, with memories organized by date.

Use Folklore feature

Records and preserve traditional beliefs, customs, stories, and cultural practices through the audio conversations using Folklores feature.

Plan family events

Plan and organize family gatherings, invite family members, and share memories from the event.

Explore the app

Continue to explore Kinfolk's features, discovering new ways to connect with family members and preserve family memories.
